Endless Pizza — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Endless Pizza mobile game ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. We respect your privacy and aim to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to operate the App.

1. Data We Store on Your Device

The App stores the following locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Your best score
  • Your cheese balance (in-game currency earned during play)
  • Pizza skins you have unlocked
  • Which pizza skin you have equipped

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera. The App does not require an account.

3. Advertising

The App displays optional rewarded video advertisements (which you may choose to watch to continue a game run after a death). These ads are served by Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC. When you watch a rewarded ad, AdMob may collect device-level identifiers (such as your Identifier for Advertisers / IDFA, if you have allowed tracking via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt), general device information, and approximate geographic region to serve ads relevant to you.

If you decline tracking via the App Tracking Transparency prompt, AdMob will only show non-personalized advertisements based on contextual signals. You may change your tracking permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on iOS.

For details on how Google uses advertising data, please see Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

5. Data Retention

Local data (scores, currency, skins) is retained on your device until you delete the App or clear its data via iOS settings. Advertising data retention is governed by Google AdMob.

6. Your Rights

Because the App stores data only locally, you can erase all data at any time by uninstalling the App. There is no server-side data to request or delete.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after a change indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
